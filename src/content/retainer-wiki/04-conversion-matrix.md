# Conversion Matrix

## Overview

The Conversion Matrix is the diagnostic tool we use to score a brand's post-click experience across all funnel layers. It's the backbone of the sales process (generates the data for Call 2) and the audit process (prioritises what to fix first).

## How It Works

Score each layer 1-10 based on the criteria below. Layers scoring below 6 are "leaking" — those get prioritised in the roadmap.

## Scoring Criteria

### Layer 1: Traffic Quality (1-10)
- Traffic source diversity (not 100% one channel)
- Audience-message match (are the right people arriving?)
- Device split health (mobile vs desktop)
- Bounce rate by source

### Layer 2: Ad-to-Page Alignment (1-10)
- Does the landing page match the ad message?
- Scent trail: headline, imagery, offer consistency
- Are they using dedicated post-click pages or generic pages?

### Layer 3: Landing Page / Homepage (1-10)
- Clear value proposition above fold
- Social proof visible without scrolling
- Single clear CTA path
- Page load speed (< 3s)
- Mobile experience

### Layer 4: Product Detail Page (1-10)
- Product imagery quality and quantity
- Reviews and social proof
- Clear pricing and offer structure
- Add-to-cart visibility and friction
- Cross-sell / upsell presence
- Trust signals (guarantees, returns, shipping)

### Layer 5: Cart (1-10)
- Cart-to-checkout rate (benchmark: 55-65%)
- Upsell/cross-sell opportunities
- Trust signals present
- Free shipping threshold
- Cart abandonment recovery

### Layer 6: Checkout (1-10)
- Checkout conversion rate (benchmark: 45-55%)
- Express checkout options (Shop Pay, Apple Pay)
- Trust badges and security signals
- Form field optimisation
- Post-purchase upsell

### Layer 7: Post-Purchase (1-10)
- Order confirmation experience
- Upsell on thank you page
- Email flow (welcome, review request, replenishment)
- Unboxing experience

### Layer 8: Retention (1-10)
- Email/SMS capture rate
- Repeat purchase rate
- Subscription offering (if applicable)
- Loyalty / rewards programme

## Interpreting Results

- **Average score 7+** — Strong post-click. Hard to justify retainer unless specific layers are weak.
- **Average score 5-7** — Good opportunity. Clear improvement path. Ideal retainer client.
- **Average score below 5** — Significant gaps. High impact potential but may need foundational work first.
