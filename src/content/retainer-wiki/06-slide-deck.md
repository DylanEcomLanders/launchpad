# Slide Deck Generator

## Overview

The Slide Deck Generator creates a branded discovery deck for Call 2 using data from the Conversion Matrix and Revenue Projector. One click, personalised to the prospect, ready to present.

## Deck Structure (8 slides)

### Slide 1: Cover
- Ecom Landers logo
- "[Brand Name] — Conversion Opportunity Analysis"
- Date

### Slide 2: The Problem
- "Your ad spend is working. Your post-click experience isn't."
- Their monthly traffic number
- Their current CVR vs benchmark
- Visual: funnel diagram with leak points highlighted

### Slide 3: Where You're Leaking
- Conversion Matrix scores by layer
- Red/amber/green visual for each layer
- Top 3 problem areas called out

### Slide 4: The Revenue Gap
- Revenue Projector output
- Monthly gap figure (prominent)
- Annual gap figure
- "This is revenue your traffic is already generating — you're just losing it after the click"

### Slide 5: What We'd Fix First
- 90-day roadmap preview (3 priorities)
- Before/after examples from similar brands
- Expected impact per change

### Slide 6: How We Work
- The retainer model explained
- Month 1: Audit + Roadmap
- Month 2+: Build, Test, Optimise, Compound
- "Not a vendor. Your conversion team."

### Slide 7: The Offer
- Core tier (primary) — £X/mo
- Pro tier (anchor) — £X/mo
- What's included in each
- Breakeven calculation: "Pays for itself at a 0.X% CVR lift"

### Slide 8: Next Steps
- "Start date: [date]"
- "First deliverable: Full audit and roadmap in 3 business days"
- Contact details

## Design Notes

- Black and white, minimal — let the numbers do the talking
- No stock photography — use their own site screenshots
- Data-heavy, copy-light — every slide has a number that matters
- Ecom Landers branding subtle — this is about them, not us
