# Sales Process

## Two-Call Close Methodology

### Call 1: The Process Call
**Goal:** Establish authority, explain the framework, get buy-in to run the analysis.

**Structure:**
1. Quick intro — who we are, what we do (2 mins)
2. Their situation — what are they spending, where is traffic going, what's converting (5 mins)
3. The framework — explain the 10-layer funnel model, how most brands leak at layers 3-6 (5 mins)
4. The offer — "We'll run your site through our conversion matrix and come back with a revenue hypothesis. No charge. If the numbers make sense, we'll talk about working together." (3 mins)

**Key phrases:**
- "We only work with brands where the maths works"
- "If your post-click experience is already optimised, we'll tell you"
- "We're not pitching — we're diagnosing"

### Between Calls: The Research Phase
This is where the toolkit does the heavy lifting.

1. **Conversion Matrix** — Run their URL through the tool, score each funnel layer
2. **Revenue Projector** — Calculate OTE based on their traffic × CVR gap × AOV
3. **Slide Deck Generator** — Auto-build a branded deck with their numbers

### Call 2: The Discovery Call
**Goal:** Present findings, show revenue gap, close.

**Structure:**
1. Recap — "Here's what we found" (2 mins)
2. Walk through the conversion matrix scores — show where they're leaking (10 mins)
3. Revenue projection — "Based on your traffic, closing this CVR gap = £X/mo in recovered revenue" (5 mins)
4. The offer — "Our retainer is £X/mo. Based on these numbers, it pays for itself at a 0.3% CVR lift. Most of our clients see 1-2% in the first 90 days." (5 mins)
5. Objection handling and close (5 mins)

## Pre-Sales Research Checklist

Before Call 2, gather:
- [ ] Traffic estimate (SimilarWeb or their GA if shared)
- [ ] Current CVR (ask on Call 1 or estimate from industry benchmarks)
- [ ] AOV (from site or ask)
- [ ] Ad spend estimate (from Meta Ad Library activity level)
- [ ] Tech stack (BuiltWith — Shopify Plus? Recharge? Klaviyo?)
- [ ] Competitor landscape (who else is doing it well in their niche)
- [ ] 2-3 specific CRO observations from their site
- [ ] Revenue hypothesis calculated and ready

## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We already have an agency" | "Great — are they doing post-click? Most agencies stop at the ad. We start where they stop." |
| "We can't commit to a retainer" | "I understand. But one-off pages don't compound. You test, learn, iterate — that's where the ROI lives." |
| "It's too expensive" | "Your current CVR gap is costing you £X/mo in lost revenue. The retainer is a fraction of that." |
| "We want to see results first" | "That's exactly what the first 90 days deliver. Audit, roadmap, first builds, first tests. If the numbers don't move, we'll have an honest conversation." |
| "Can we start smaller?" | "We can — but a half-effort gets half-results. The reason this works is we own the full post-click experience, not just one page." |
