# Positioning & Language

## Core Positioning Statement

"Ecom Landers is the post-click conversion engine for DTC brands. We don't build pages — we build revenue."

## What We Say vs What We Don't

| Say | Don't Say |
|-----|-----------|
| Conversion engine | Agency |
| Your CRO team | Your vendor |
| Revenue recovered | Potential uplift |
| Post-click experience | Landing pages |
| Compounding results | One-off projects |
| Diagnosis and roadmap | Audit and report |
| Retainer investment | Monthly cost |
| Conversion partner | Service provider |

## Elevator Pitches

### 10-second version
"We're the conversion team for DTC brands. You drive the traffic, we make sure it converts."

### 30-second version
"Most DTC brands spend £50-100K/mo driving traffic to pages that don't convert. We plug in as their full CRO team — audit the funnel, build the pages, run the tests, compound the results. Our retainer pays for itself inside the first 60 days for most clients."

### 60-second version (for Call 1)
"Here's what we see with most brands doing £80-120K/mo: they've figured out the traffic side. Meta's working, Google's working, maybe TikTok's getting traction. But when someone clicks the ad, they land on a generic collection page or a PDP that hasn't been touched in 18 months. That's where the money leaks. We come in as the full post-click team — we audit every layer from landing page through checkout, build a prioritised roadmap, then execute monthly: new pages, tests, optimisations. It compounds. Month over month your CVR climbs, your ROAS improves, and your CAC drops. Most of our clients see it pay for itself within 60 days."

## Social Proof Framework

When sharing results:
- Lead with the metric: "2.1% → 4.3% CVR in 90 days"
- Follow with the context: "DTC supplements brand, £100K/mo revenue"
- Close with the method: "3 landing page builds + checkout optimisation"
- Never name the client unless they've approved it

1. "Your ads aren't the problem. Your landing pages are."
2. "One page build doesn't compound. A conversion programme does."
3. "You wouldn't hire a doctor for one appointment."
4. "The brands scaling past 8 figures aren't testing button colours."
5. "Your post-click experience is either making money or losing it. There's no neutral."
