# Master CRO Audit Checklist

Use this checklist when auditing a DTC ecommerce store. Walk through each layer, score each item, and identify the highest-impact opportunities.

## Scoring System

For each question, score the store:

- **1 (Critical)** — Broken, missing, or actively harmful. Fix immediately.
- **2 (Poor)** — Exists but poorly executed. High-priority improvement.
- **3 (Acceptable)** — Functional but not optimised. Medium priority.
- **4 (Good)** — Well-executed, minor improvements possible.
- **5 (Excellent)** — Best-in-class. Don't touch it.

**Layer Score** = Average of all questions in that layer. Any layer scoring below 3.0 is a priority target.

### Priority Framework
1. Fix anything scoring 1 (Critical) immediately — these are actively costing revenue
2. Address all layers scoring below 3.0 as high-priority projects
3. Optimise layers scoring 3.0-4.0 through structured testing
4. Leave layers scoring above 4.0 alone (don't fix what isn't broken)

### Next Steps Template
After completing the audit, document:
1. **Top 3 Critical Issues** — What's actively broken and costing revenue right now?
2. **Top 5 Quick Wins** — What can be fixed in < 1 week with significant impact?
3. **Top 3 Strategic Projects** — What needs a structured testing program over 1-3 months?
4. **What NOT to Touch** — What's working well and should be left alone?
