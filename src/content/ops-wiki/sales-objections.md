# Objection Bank — Position & Proof

Every objection we hit on sales calls, with a one-line position and a proof line to back it up. Keep adding as new ones come in — this is a living doc.

---

## "£8K is too expensive"

**Position:** You're not buying a service, you're buying recovered revenue. The number that matters is what it returns, not what it costs.

**Proof:** Our average client recovers 4–6x in the first 90 days. If we don't, month 4 is notice — you risk £24K, not a year.

---

## "We already have an agency"

**Position:** Great — who's the conversion layer? Ads agencies don't own CVR. Email agencies don't own PDPs. That gap is what we fill.

**Proof:** Half our clients run us alongside their ad agency. They're the ones who actually compound results.

---

## "We want to build in-house"

**Position:** You should. We accelerate that — by month 6 you'll have the playbook, the test history, and the data to hire against.

**Proof:** Three of our retainers have transitioned roadmap ownership in-house after 12 months. Our job is to make ourselves optional.

---

## "How do I know it'll work?"

**Position:** You don't — which is why we run 90-day sprints, not annual contracts.

**Proof:** 30-day notice from month 4. If the numbers don't move by month 3, we've failed and you leave. No fight.

---

## "We've been burned by agencies before"

**Position:** Every brand over £500K has been. What were you promised, and what actually shipped?

**Proof (after listening):** The difference is cadence and visibility — you'll see every test, every result, every week. Nothing hidden.

---

## "We don't have the traffic yet"

**Position:** Then you shouldn't hire us yet. Come back when you're at £[X] monthly ad spend — we're a waste of money below that.

**Proof:** This is a disqualifier, not an objection. Using it builds trust. Log them, follow up in 6 months.

---

## "Can we start smaller?"

**Position:** Yes — but smaller means one-off page, not monthly. We don't do retainers under £8K because the rhythm doesn't compound.

**Proof:** If you want to start with a single £5–8K build, we can do that, and credit the fee against month one of a retainer if you convert.

---

## "How is this different from [competitor]?"

**Position (after listening):** They build pages. We own outcomes. Their business model depends on you briefing them. Ours depends on us getting results.

**Proof:** Ask them what happens if your CVR doesn't move in 90 days. Then ask us.

---

## "We need to think about it"

**Position:** Of course. What specifically is the thing you're not sure about — the price, the fit, or the team?

**Proof:** Never let "think about it" close the call. Get the real objection first. Nine times out of ten it's one of the objections above in disguise.

---

## "Can we see more case studies?"

**Position:** Yes. Which vertical / price point / traffic source matches you? Better to show one relevant than ten generic.

**Proof:** Send 2–3 tailored. Never the full deck — saturation kills belief.

---

## Add to this doc

When you hear something new on a call, jot it down. Pattern goes:

- **Position** — one line, punchy, reframes their concern
- **Proof** — concrete number / fact / commitment that backs the position
