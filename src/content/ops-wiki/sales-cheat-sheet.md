# Sales Cheat Sheet

One-page internal reference for the Conversion Partnership pitch. Use on sales calls, reference before the deep-dive audit, send to anyone on the team running discovery.

---

## Opening line (warm lead)

> "Most Shopify brands have an ads team, an email team, and nobody owning conversion. We're the team that sits between traffic and revenue."

---

## Discovery — four questions that do the selling for you

1. What's your average monthly ad spend, and what's your blended CVR?
2. Who owns your conversion roadmap today — you or an agency?
3. Last 5 landing pages / PDPs you shipped — who briefed them, who built them, what did they lift?
4. If a 0.5% CVR lift at your current traffic is worth £[X]/month, does that change how you'd budget for it?

The right prospect answers these with tension. If they don't, they're not ready.

---

## The two-stage audit

- **Stage 1 (free, 20 min):** high-level reveal — "here are 3–5 places you're leaking." Quick, visual, not a proposal.
- **Stage 2 (paid or credited):** deep dive — conversion matrix, custom roadmap, real numbers. Close happens here.

---

## Framing the price

- Never "£8K/month". Always **"£8K against £[X] of monthly recovered revenue"**.
- If they compare to freelancers: _"Freelancers build. We own the result. Different job."_
- If they compare to in-house: _"Hiring a full CRO team is £25K+/mo. We're £8K and already assembled."_

---

## Close script

> "What I'd propose is a 90-day partnership. Month one we ship [the thing they've told you they need]. By month three we've tested against it. If the numbers don't move, you haven't locked in — it's 30-day notice from month four. Does that feel workable?"

---

## Red flags — do not sign

- Sub-£500K/year revenue
- No ad spend / no paid traffic
- Already married to another CRO agency
- Can't name a decision-maker on the call
- Wants "just a page built" and nothing else — send them to flow-02-design-dev

---

## Language: what we are / what we aren't

| We are | We aren't |
|---|---|
| A conversion partner | A vendor |
| Strategy + execution owners | Page-builders for hire |
| Accountable to CVR / AOV / RPV numbers | Paid per deliverable |
| The team that sits next to ads + email | The team underneath ads + email |

---

## Post-call follow-up

- Send the Stage 1 audit within 24 hours
- If the prospect booked Stage 2, include the calendar link + what to prep (GA4 view access, Shopify collaborator)
- If no Stage 2 booked, send one case study tailored to their vertical, then stop
