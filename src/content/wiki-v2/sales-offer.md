What we sell, why it's the only thing we sell, and how it's priced.

## The Conversion Engine

The **Conversion Engine is the offer**. It's the one thing we lead with, and the one thing we want every new client signing onto. It's a partnership-level commitment to the brand's conversion layer, not a project, not a vendor deliverable.

Why this is the headline offer:

- It compounds. Lifts stack across the funnel over time.
- It produces our best case studies and wins.
- It positions us as the funnel agency rather than another build shop.

## What's in scope

Holistic work on the conversion layer:

- **Design** — landing pages, product pages, funnel assets.
- **Development** — clean custom Shopify, fast and accountable.
- **Strategy** — hypotheses, conversion mapping, testing logic.
- **Testing** — A/B and before/after measurement, with weekly reporting.

We're not a content writing agency, a paid media buyer, or an email service provider. Anything outside the funnel layer is out of scope unless we explicitly bring in a partner.

## Pricing

Two retainer tiers:

| Tier | Monthly |
| --- | --- |
| Standard | £8,000 |
| Premium | £12,000 |

The **£12K is the anchor**. It's the right number for clients who want more bandwidth, but its existence makes the £8K feel like the obvious starting point for most.

## Commitment

The minimum commitment is the **90-day Conversion Engine cycle**. Anything shorter doesn't give us enough room to ship strategy, build, and a full test loop. After 90 days clients renew month-to-month.

## How it scales

The plan is to push pricing up as the case study library grows. Target step-ups at **5 active retainers**, **10 active**, and **15 active**. Same offer, sharper proof, higher number.
