The one-page reference for any sales conversation. Use before discovery calls, between calls, and to onboard anyone new to running them.

## Opening line

> **Most Shopify brands have an ads team, an email team, and nobody owning conversion. We're the team that sits between traffic and revenue.**

The rest of the conversation flows from this framing.

## Four discovery questions that do the selling for you

The questions are designed to surface tension. The right prospect answers them with friction. The wrong prospect answers neutrally — log them and move on.

1. What's your average monthly ad spend, and what's your blended CVR?
2. Who owns your conversion roadmap today — you or an agency?
3. The last five landing pages or PDPs you shipped — who briefed them, who built them, what did they lift?
4. If a 0.5% CVR lift at your current traffic is worth £[X]/month, does that change how you'd budget for it?

If question 3 produces a blank stare, you're talking to the right person.

## The two-stage audit framing

| Stage | What | Time |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Stage 1 (free) | High-level reveal. "Here are 3-5 places you're leaking" | 20 min |
| Stage 2 (paid or credited) | Deep dive. Conversion Matrix, custom roadmap, real numbers | The close happens here |

Stage 1 is the wedge. Stage 2 is where they sign.

## Framing the price

Never quote £8K/month in isolation. Always anchor it to revenue:

- **"£8K against £[X] of monthly recovered revenue."**
- If they compare to freelancers: *"Freelancers build. We own the result. Different job."*
- If they compare to in-house: *"Hiring a full CRO team is £25K+/mo. We're £8K and already assembled."*

## Close script

> "What I'd propose is a 90-day partnership. Month one we ship [the thing they've told you they need]. By month three we've tested against it. If the numbers don't move, you haven't locked in. It's 30-day notice from month four. Does that feel workable?"

## Red flags — do not sign

- Sub-£500K/year revenue
- No ad spend or no paid traffic
- Already married to another CRO agency
- Can't name a decision-maker on the call
- Wants "just a page built" and nothing else (send them to a one-off project, not a retainer)

## Language: what we are, what we aren't

| We are | We aren't |
| --- | --- |
| A conversion partner | A vendor |
| Strategy + execution owners | Page-builders for hire |
| Accountable to CVR / AOV / RPV numbers | Paid per deliverable |
| The team that sits next to ads + email | The team underneath ads + email |

## Post-call follow-up

- [ ] Send the Stage 1 audit within 24 hours
- [ ] If they booked Stage 2: include the calendar link + what to prep (GA4 view access, Shopify collaborator)
- [ ] If no Stage 2 booked: send one case study tailored to their vertical, then stop. Saturation kills belief.
