Every landing page project follows the same design workflow regardless of scope. This keeps quality consistent, timelines predictable, and clients confident. No design work begins without a signed-off brief, and no design goes to dev without client approval.

## The flow

| Step | What | Output |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1. Brief received | Account lead reviews for completeness (brand assets, goals, audience, competitors). Missing pieces are requested before assigning. | Complete brief, designer assigned |
| 2. Wireframe | Low-fi greyscale in Figma. Information hierarchy, section order, CTA placement. Desktop AND mobile, never desktop only. | Shared with client for structural feedback |
| 3. High-fidelity | Apply brand colours, typography, imagery, tone. Real copy only. Desktop and mobile simultaneously. Hover states + animations annotated. | Ready for internal review |
| 4. Internal review | Peer review by another designer or the design lead. Run through the checklist below. | Fix before client sees it |
| 5. Client review | Loom walkthrough, never a raw Figma link. Two revision rounds in standard scope. Third triggers a scope conversation. | Approved or revisions noted |
| 6. Handoff to dev | Follow the Design to Dev Handoff process. | Dev queue |

## Design review checklist

Before any design goes to client review:

- [ ] Desktop and mobile designs both complete
- [ ] All copy is real, not placeholder
- [ ] Brand colours and fonts match the style guide
- [ ] CTAs prominent and above the fold on mobile
- [ ] Social proof uses real reviews / testimonials
- [ ] Images are high quality and correctly cropped
- [ ] Spacing consistent on the 8px grid
- [ ] Text contrast passes WCAG AA (4.5:1 minimum)
- [ ] Hover and interaction states documented
- [ ] One primary CTA per viewport

## Timeline standards

| Phase | Standard | Rush |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Wireframe | 1 business day | Same day |
| High-fidelity | 2-3 business days | 1-2 business days |
| Internal review | 0.5 business days | Same day |
| Client review round | 1-2 business days | 1 business day |
| Revisions per round | 1 business day | Same day |
| Handoff prep | 0.5 business days | Same day |

Total typical brief → approved design: **5-7 business days**. Rush projects can compress to 3 days but require the client to respond within hours, not days.

## What causes delays

- Missing brand assets at brief stage
- Placeholder copy that needs to be written
- Multiple stakeholders giving conflicting feedback
- Scope creep disguised as "small tweaks"

Flag these the same day you see them. Don't wait.
