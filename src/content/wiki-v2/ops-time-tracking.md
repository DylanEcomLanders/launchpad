We don't time-track everything. The team is mostly outcome-based. The one exception is development hours, because those numbers matter for capacity and payment balance.

## What gets tracked

**Dev hours.** If a developer says something will take X hours, those hours get logged through the **Dev Hours module on the Team Hub**.

Everything else (design, strategy, PM work) is deliverables-based and not tracked by the clock.

## Why we track dev time

Three reasons:

- **Capacity** — knowing how much dev time a pod has spent informs how much it has left.
- **Client allocation** — shows where we're under- or over-investing per client, and where we could offer more.
- **Payment balance** — ensures dev pay and dev invoicing reconcile against what was actually done.

## What we do with the data

Right now it's for reference, not enforcement. We look at accrued time to spot inefficiencies and to plan ahead. It isn't used to micromanage anyone's day.
