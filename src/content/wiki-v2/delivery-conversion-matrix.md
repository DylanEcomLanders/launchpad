The Conversion Matrix is the diagnostic tool that powers everything. It scores a brand's post-click experience across every funnel layer, identifies revenue leaks, and produces the numbers that both sell the partnership and prioritise the roadmap.

> **Two purposes, one tool. Sales uses it to quantify the gap. Delivery uses it to decide what to fix first.**

## Evidence vs proof

Critical distinction:

- **Evidence** = what we can observe from the outside (Stage 1 audit, public data, ad clicks, on-site checks)
- **Proof** = what the data confirms with backend access (Stage 2 audit, real GA4 numbers, Shopify analytics)

Stage 1 gives evidence. Stage 2 gives proof. Both feed into the matrix; proof is what closes the deal.

Framing on a prospect call:

- "Based on what we can see publicly, your landing pages are scoring a 4/10" → evidence
- "Your GA4 shows a 68% bounce rate on mobile PDPs and a 1.2% site-wide CVR against a 3.1% category benchmark" → proof

## Revenue projection from scores

Once scored, calculate the revenue impact:

```
Monthly traffic × Current CVR = Current monthly conversions
Monthly traffic × Benchmark CVR = Potential monthly conversions
(Potential - Current) × AOV = Monthly revenue gap
```

Then layer in ad spend:

```
Monthly ad spend ÷ Current conversions = Current CPA
Monthly ad spend ÷ Potential conversions = Potential CPA
Current CPA - Potential CPA = CPA savings per acquisition
```

**Frame to the client:** "You're spending £50K/month on ads generating 150,000 sessions. At your current 1.8% CVR you get 2,700 orders. At the benchmark 3.2% you'd get 4,800 orders. That's 2,100 extra orders × £55 AOV = **£115,500/month you're missing.** And your CPA drops from £18.50 to £10.40."

## The 8 layers

Score each 1-10.

### 1. Traffic quality

Traffic source diversity. Audience-message match. Device split health. Bounce rate by source. Session quality (pages per session, time on site).

### 2. Ad-to-page alignment

Does the landing page match the ad message? (scent trail). Headline + imagery + offer consistency from ad → page. Dedicated post-click pages or generic collection/home? Different pages for different audiences?

### 3. Landing page / homepage

Clear value prop above fold (understandable in 3 seconds). Social proof visible without scrolling. Single clear CTA path. Page load under 3s on mobile. Purpose-built mobile experience. Copy quality (benefits over features).

### 4. Product detail page

Imagery quality and quantity (lifestyle + detail + scale). Reviews (volume, recency, quality). Clear pricing and offer structure. ATC visibility and friction. Cross-sell / upsell presence. Trust signals (guarantees, returns, shipping, security). Product description quality.

### 5. Cart experience

Cart-to-checkout rate (benchmark 55-65%). Upsell / cross-sell in cart. Trust signals present. Free shipping threshold and progress bar. Cart abandonment recovery. Friction points (forced account creation, surprise costs).

### 6. Checkout

Checkout conversion rate (benchmark 45-55%). Express checkout (Shop Pay, Apple Pay, Google Pay). Trust badges. Form field optimisation. Shipping options and transparency. Post-purchase one-click upsell.

### 7. Post-purchase

Order confirmation page (upsell or dead end?). Thank you page (cross-sell, referral, social sharing). Post-purchase email flow. Unboxing experience. Post-purchase upsell conversion.

### 8. Retention & LTV

Email / SMS capture rate. Repeat purchase rate. Subscription offering (if consumable). Loyalty programme. Win-back flow for lapsed customers. Customer segmentation (VIP treatment).

## Interpreting results

| Avg score | Verdict | Action |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **7+** | Strong post-click. Limited room for improvement. | Hard to justify a full partnership unless 1-2 layers are critically weak. Consider project-based engagement. |
| **5-7** | Good opportunity. Clear improvement path. | **Ideal partnership client.** Multiple levers to pull, enough traffic to test, clear ROI path. |
| **Below 5** | Significant gaps across the funnel. | High impact potential but may need foundational work first. Great partnership candidate if traffic + budget are there. |

## Quick-score cheat sheet (Stage 1 / public data)

| Layer | Quick check | How to score without analytics |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Traffic | SimilarWeb traffic estimate | High traffic = likely decent quality |
| Ad-to-page | Click their ads, check the landing page | Match or mismatch? |
| Landing / home | Above fold | Value prop clear in 3 seconds? |
| PDP | Top product pages | Reviews? Trust? CTA visible? |
| Cart | Add to cart, check experience | Upsells? Friction? Threshold? |
| Checkout | Start checkout, don't complete | Express checkout? Form fields? |
| Post-purchase | Can't check without buying | Assume 4/10 unless referral / reviews visible |
| Retention | Email popup, loyalty page | Popup? Programme visible? |
