How money moves in and out of Ecomlanders.

## Invoicing clients

Retainers are billed **monthly**. Client invoices go out in time to be paid by month-end.

## Team invoices

The team submits their own invoices.

- **Retained roles** — monthly invoice, submitted **by the 26th** of the month, paid by end of month.
- **Deliverables-based roles** — invoice once the deliverable has landed. **Don't invoice for work that isn't finished.**

Submit through the team invoice flow in Launchpad (`/team/invoice`).

## Expenses

Expenses are handled case by case. If something is genuinely needed to do the work, message **Dylan** and he'll sort it. There's no formal reimbursement form. Keep the receipt and the conversation in Slack.

## Rates

A reference of everyone's rates is maintained inside Launchpad behind a password. Founders and the relevant Heads have access; the rest of the team doesn't.
