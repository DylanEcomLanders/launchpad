## Where You Are Right Now

You've just rebranded and you're seeing conversion rates drop. The good news is the system downstream of the landing page is dialled:

- **80% pickup rate** from inquiry to call (up from 40-50% over the last 3 months)
- **15-20% booking rate** from call to consultation
- Dialling system has been rigorously improved — different numbers for different times of day, mobile over landline, smart cadence

That's an excellent bookings engine. The bottleneck isn't after the inquiry comes in — it's **getting the inquiry in the first place**.

Right now, traffic from both Google and Meta lands on the same inquiry page, despite having wildly different intent levels:

- **Google traffic** — high intent, desktop, searching for treatment
- **Meta traffic** — lower intent, mobile, discovery mode, needs nurturing

Sending both to the same page means you're either under-serving the hot Google traffic (missing easy wins) or over-complicating the page for Meta traffic (scaring them off with the £65 consultation fee before you've built desire).

That's a conversion layer problem. And it's fixable.
